# The Yaartri Manifesto: Radical Candor for Travelers

At Yaartri, we believe that high-trust exploration is built on **Radical Candor** and **Accountability**. We are not a passive social network; we are an active, agent-driven sanctuary where your word is your bond. 

To maintain the integrity of our Aadhaar-verified ecosystem, every "Yaar" must adhere to these core principles. The Yaartri Agent enforces these rules to ensure everyone's safety and economic efficiency.

## 1. Respect the RSVP (The Anti-Ghosting Law)
**"A commitment made is a commitment kept."**
*   **The Problem:** Ghosting ruins the logistics of shared transport and group tables.
*   **The Yaartri Standard:** If you commit to an "Evening Aarti Squad" or a shared Rickshaw, you show up.
*   **The Yaartri Enforcement:**
    *   If you are running late, the Agent will prompt you to update the group.
    *   If you ghost without warning, Yaartri will apply a "Ghosting Penalty" to your **Yaar Score**, dropping your visibility for future high-trust squads.

## 2. Default to Transparency
**"Surprises are for birthdays, not travel logistics."**
*   **The Problem:** Ambiguity in intent, whether romantic or financial, degrades trust.
*   **The Yaartri Standard:** Be explicitly clear about what you are seeking—whether it's splitting a cab fare or finding a quiet co-working partner. Do not turn a "Squad-Up" into an unsolicited date.
*   **The Yaartri Enforcement:** Yaartri operates a **Sentiment Sentinel**. High-tension or un-Yaar-like text in the group chat will trigger a polite, private intervention from the Agent, and repeated infractions result in shadow-banning.

## 3. Leave Nobody Behind (The Table-Share Principle)
**"We share the space; we share the cost."**
*   **The Problem:** Solo travelers often face the "Dinner for One" blues and unfair single-rider surcharges.
*   **The Yaartri Standard:** If you have an empty seat at your table at Bar Palladio, or an empty seat in your Shikara, open it to the verified network.
*   **The Yaartri Enforcement:** Yaartri will handle the awkwardness of the **Smart-Split UPI**. When the activity concludes, the Agent handles the math so you can focus on the memory.

## 4. Protect the Sanctuary
**"Safety is a collective responsibility."**
*   **The Problem:** Tourist traps and bad actors target solo explorers.
*   **The Yaartri Standard:** Prioritize meeting at Yaartri-designated "Safe-Hubs" (verified cafes, government-approved sites) and stay within the "Aadhaar-Lite" trust loop.
*   **The Yaartri Enforcement:** Yaartri filters out non-verified entities. If you attempt to override safety boundaries, the Agent will challenge your decision ("Are you sure you want to leave the Safe-Hub network?").
